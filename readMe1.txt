 stores goods to sell

 Customers
 transaction
 Item
 Cart
 
Table relationship
1. A customer can have many transaction and a transaction can have one customer.Hence
  Customer and Transaction has one to many relationship.
2. A transaction can have many items and an item can be in many transactions.Hence transaction 
   and item has many to many relationship.
3. A cart can have many items and an item can be in many carts.Hence cart and item have many to many relationship
4. A customer can have one Cart.A cart can have one customer.Hence Cart and customer have one to one relationship.
